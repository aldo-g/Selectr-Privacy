Selectr Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 November 2025

Selectr does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Spotify Account Access

Selectr uses Spotify’s official OAuth login. Your credentials are handled securely by Spotify and are never accessible to the app. Selectr only requests the minimum permissions needed to read and update playlists and liked songs based on your actions.

Advertising

Selectr uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as IDFA) to show relevant ads. This data is handled according to Google’s privacy policy and is never stored by Selectr.

Data Retention

Selectr does not store or transmit any user data. All operations occur directly with Spotify’s API.
